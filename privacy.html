Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Electron Game Studios ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile games (the "Apps").

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to play.

When ads are shown, ad partners may collect device and usage data to serve ads.

Advertising (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob and its partners may collect information such as device identifiers, approximate location, and app activity to provide ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.

We currently serve non-personalized ads where applicable. If you are in regions that require consent (such as the EEA/UK), we will request consent before serving personalized ads or using cookies/identifiers as required by law.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

We do not currently use third-party analytics or crash reporting SDKs. If we add analytics or crash reporting in the future, we will update this policy and our store disclosures before the change takes effect.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with ad partners to serve ads, measure performance, and maintain the safety and integrity of the Apps.

Children

Our Apps are not directed to children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Your Choices

You can limit ad tracking or reset your device advertising ID in your device settings. You can also uninstall the App at any time.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at electron.cyber.secure@gmail.com.

This policy may be updated from time to time. We will post changes on this page.
